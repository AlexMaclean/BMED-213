Impacts

research

The potential impacts of targeted proteomic assays using triple quadrupole mass spectrometers are large. The technology is already largely used in research labs and clinical trials when researchers work to better understand the mechanisms and progression of diseases.

Clinical

If the cost of triple quadrupole mass spectrometers comes down over the next few years, they could be used widescale by hospitals to identify abnormal protein levels associated with diseases. Allowing the health care system to catch and treat diseases like cancer earlier.
